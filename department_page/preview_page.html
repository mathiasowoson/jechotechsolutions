Our Projects Done.

Take a look at some of the successful projects we've completed. From electrical installations to smart systems, we deliver excellence. Each project reflects our passion, precision, and professionalism.

A smart mini vehicle controlled with a DIV remote. It helps students learn wireless communication and motor control. Great for teaching the basics of robotics and remote systems.

This speedy race car responds to a regular TV remote. It introduces infrared (IR) sensor technology in a fun way. Perfect for hands-on learning about speed control and sensor input.

A rugged 4-wheel bot designed to mimic a Mars exploration rover. Built to handle tough terrain while collecting mock data. Ideal for learning sensors, autonomous driving, and space tech.

A floating boat that sails on water using Arduino control. Teaches waterproof circuit design and propeller mechanics. Used for fun demonstrations and marine tech learning.

A creative battle tank model with match-powered "firing" effects. Combines mechanics and safety-controlled ignition. Used to simulate defense systems and military tech concepts.

A lightweight flying jet model built with cotton and electronics. Demonstrates aerodynamics, propeller thrust, and balance. Great for STEM learning and aviation design practice.
